About Us

Altura Hotel is located in the beautiful mountains of the cloud forest that surrounds the Poas Volcano. It is 45 minutes away from the Juan Santamar칤a Airport making our place convenient starting your trip or ending it here. Our hotel is only 5 minutes away from the National Park and has spectacular views of the Central Valley. Due to our exclusive secluded location in the countryside you will be able to relax, reconnect to nature, and to yourself. While visiting us you can enjoy our gardens and nature trails, as well as getting to know all of our friendly farm animals. We will make you feel like home.

Hotel Facilities

Viewpoint

In the hotel we offer a space where you can enjoy spectacular views of the central valley. Particularly clear nights becoming somtimes the ideal place to hear the howling of the coyotes.

Fireplace

At 5:30pm our bonfire is turned on allowing our guests to enjoy the beautiful scenery with a much nicer temperature. The fireplace is mainly an attraction to the smaller ones because it can be the perfect place for marshmallow roasting.

Walking trails

We dedicated a piece of the property to the reforestation of species of Poasito. During your stay you will be able to enjoy the trails and the animals that live within the property.

Breakfast

Typical Breakfast is included while staying with us. Everyday our guests can enjoy it between 8am-10:00am.

Wine, beers, and soft drinks

For your convenience we have at your disposal wine, costarican beers, and soft drinks.

Laundry

Altura Hotel offers its guests laundry service. Deliver the items you need to wash and we take care of it.
